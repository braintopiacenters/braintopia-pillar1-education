\# Braintopia Brand Guidelines



\## Primary Colors (use these exactly)

\- Primary Blue: #40cdfc

\- Accent Yellow: #ffdf06

\- Warm Accent Orange/Red: #ff2b32

\- Background / Light: #F8F9FA

\- Text Dark: #272727

\- Success / Positive: #27AE60



\## Logo

\- Main logo file: assets/braintopia-logo.png

\- Use this logo on the header, results screens, and key places.

\- Keep good whitespace around it.



\## Tone

\- Warm, supportive, empowering, and lightly adventurous.

\- Professional medical feel with a friendly touch.

\- Never gimmicky or childish.
