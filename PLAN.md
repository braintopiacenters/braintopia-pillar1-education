\# Braintopia Pillar 1 App Specification



\## Project Overview

Build a clean, modern, mobile-first web application focused on \*\*Pillar 1: Education \& Family Support\*\* at Braintopia.



The app uses 7 specific audience classes (The Guide, The Caregiver, The Learner, The Bridge, The Rebalancer, The Different Thinker, and The Supporter). Each class represents real people connected to teaching, parenting, learning, focus, regulation, and family support.



\*\*Goal:\*\* Create an engaging, professional tool that helps visitors discover which class they most identify with, learn how neurofeedback can support them, and move toward booking a QEEG assessment or starting training at Braintopia.



\*\*Tone:\*\* Warm, empathetic, empowering, and lightly adventurous. Professional but approachable. Never gimmicky or childish. Blend short, respectful flavor text with grounded explanations of brain health and neurofeedback.
